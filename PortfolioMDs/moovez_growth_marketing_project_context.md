This project should be presented as an early-career growth marketing and campaign operations project completed while in school. It should not be framed as a senior marketing leadership project. The strongest angle is that it shows early hands-on experience with digital acquisition, paid media, outbound email, organic content, SEO, and analytics.

This project is useful because it shows the foundation behind later work in marketing science, RevOps, CRM automation, analytics, and product growth.

## Business / Marketing Problem
Moovez needed to generate awareness, engagement, and leads through digital channels while maintaining a practical, measurable marketing workflow. Marketing activity was spread across organic social, email newsletters, outbound email, Facebook campaigns, Google Ads, and SEO. The challenge was to keep campaigns organized, understand which channels produced engagement, and support sales activity with consistent outbound and paid media execution.

Key challenges:

- Building awareness through organic and paid channels.
- Keeping email campaigns and newsletters consistent.
- Managing outbound email pipelines.
- Improving engagement across organic social channels.
- Supporting paid media through Facebook and Google campaigns.
- Tracking campaign performance while learning digital analytics.
- Connecting marketing activity to sales and lead-generation goals.

## Solution Overview
The project involved supporting a practical growth marketing system for Moovez. The system combined email marketing, organic social, paid media, SEO, outbound pipelines, and basic analytics into an early-stage marketing workflow.

Core components:

- **Email newsletters:** Used for audience engagement, updates, and lead nurturing.
- **Outbound email pipelines:** Supported direct sales and marketing outreach.
- **Organic social media:** Used to build awareness and engagement.
- **Facebook campaigns:** Paid media channel for targeted acquisition and promotion.
- **Google Ads:** Search / paid acquisition channel.
- **SEO:** Supported organic discoverability and digital presence.
- **Analytics / reporting:** Used to understand engagement, campaign performance, and channel results.

Simple workflow:

> Content / Campaign Idea → Email / Organic / Paid Channel Execution → Engagement Tracking → Lead or Reply Activity → Campaign Learning → Next Iteration
